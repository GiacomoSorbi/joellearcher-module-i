Lorna Archer is a Holistic Wellbeing Therapist in London and the founder of Wellbeing Well YOU since January 2019. She has had a passion for wellness and the holistic world for many years and aspired to start her journey into wellness and supporting people. Lorna has over 32 years corporate financial services experience within Human Resources and People management. An interest in developing strong relationships is the foundation of what she does, both personally and professionally. Also a desire to help, support, listen and provide guidance helped her to transition from the corporate industry into the wellbeing realm. Wellbeing Well YOU specialize in providing therapies which support mind, body and soul including massage therapy, wellbeing mentoring, wellbeing/self-care workshops and meditation support through group or one-to-one sessions. Our mission is to empower, encourage and educate individuals to take ownership of their wellbeing and provide support with tools, guidance and advice on how to manage stress, bring balance and harmony to their body and help individuals create lifestyle changes to enhance their wellbeing and self-care. In her spare time, Lorna enjoys spending time with her two grown-up daughters, daily yoga and meditation, salsa dancing, walking along Southbank, travelling overseas and spa pampering. Lorna is contactable via email or Instagram.
